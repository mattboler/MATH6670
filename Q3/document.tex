\section{Q1}

Let $X$ have $\Gamma(\alpha = 3, \theta = 3)$ distribution.
Find $EX^3$ by:

\begin{itemize}
	\item $M(t) = Ee^{tX}$
	\item Smart integration
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Answer}

\subsubsection{Solution through moment generating function}

From our pdf 
\begin{equation}
	f_x = \frac{1}{\Gamma(\alpha) \theta^{\alpha}} X^{\alpha-1}e^-\frac{x}{\theta}
\end{equation}
